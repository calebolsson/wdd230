Audience Page

Home Design Purpose

Audience

The audience of the Chamber of Commerce page will include people who want to learn about what the chamber does, contact the people in the chamber, or read about some of the recent events involving the chamber.

    Personas

  • Member of the community who wants to interact in some way with the chamber
  • Local business owner who is invested in the economic outcomes of the chamber

    Scenarios

  1. A member of the community is interested in enagaging with their local economy, and wants to learn more about the role of the chamber
  2. A member of the community wants to ask a question to a member of the chamber, and is looking for names and contact information
  3. A member of the community is considering joining the chamber, and is researching how to try to join the chamber in the next cycle
  4. A local business owner wants to read about recent changes that might impact themself or their busniess
  5. A local business owner thinks that they have some important information that should be shared with the entire chamber, and is looking for a way to be heard
