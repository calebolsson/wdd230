Carlsbad

Welcome to our home! Here in Carlsbad we love to play in the sun. Some of our most exciting attractions include our gorgeous beaches, LEGO Land CA, and of course our very own Smoothie Shacks that you can find on most boardwalks!

Our story

We started as one single food stand near South Carlsbad State Beach. Thanks to the generous support of all of you smoothie fans we now have 3 locations across southern cali. Come visit us in our original shop right here in Carlsbad to learn more about our history!

California

We became the 31st state to join the Union on September 9th, 1850. Since then, California has flourished! Known for its unique experience, California offers interests for all ages. From smoothies to wine, clubs to parks, and beautiful scenery both natural and man-made.

Destinations

If you haven't already been to Disney or Beverly Hills, visiting those iconic locations is a must. Rest between adventures at any of the hotels and resorts along the coast, and don't forget to try some Sonoma coast wine (or Carlsbad smoothies) before you go home!
